# CircularLed
CircularLedger is an open-source blockchain infrastructure designed to improve transparency and accountability in decentralized waste-to-energy systems.

The platform will enable:

• Tracking of organic waste flows from households to treatment facilities
• Smart contract-based payments to waste collectors
• Real-time environmental impact dashboards
• Open digital infrastructure for circular economy systems

The project is currently at the concept stage and will develop a prototype with support from the UNICEF Venture Fund.

Lead organization: Waste Bangladesh
Website: https://wastebd.org
The platform enables:

• Tracking of organic waste flows from households to treatment facilities  
• Smart contract–based payments for waste collectors  
• Real-time environmental impact monitoring  
• Transparent data dashboards for municipalities and development partners  

CircularLedger aims to support circular economy systems that convert organic waste into clean energy and biofertilizer while improving livelihoods of informal waste workers.

## Current Status

The project is currently at the concept stage. With support from the UNICEF Venture Fund, the team plans to develop a working prototype including blockchain architecture, smart contracts, mobile data collection tools, and a public impact dashboard.
